Aloha, I’m Michelle.

My home is Hilo, Hawaiʻi.

Background

A one-time philosophy teacher, I've turned my love for creativity and logic to graphic design and web development, which I find immensely more satisfying and useful. And though I live now in the relaxed environment of a tropical island in the middle of the Pacific Ocean, I've spent most of my life in the Houston area, Chicago, and the Silicon Valley, and have had the opportunity to travel abroad frequently. I look forward to a career that will keep me connected to the world at large!

I currently am in charge of graphic design for the advertising in Hawaiʻi's Ke Ola Magazine, and also support and maintain their website and social media presence. Otherwise, I enjoy tons of things: playing guitar, swimming, yoga, tabletop games, sewing, marathoning awesome fun sci-fi TV shows, and cooking.

Goals

I want to master the process of building web sites and increase my knowledge, skills and abilities in:

  • HTML
  • CSS
  • WordPress
  • JavaScript

  • AJAX

I’d like to run my own business offering graphic design and website development to help my clients look their best!
